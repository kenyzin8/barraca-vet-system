About

Barraca Veterinary Clinic


Barraca Veterinary Clinic is a beacon of hope for pet owners, serving as a one-stop solution for their beloved pets' healthcare needs. Nestled in the city's heart, the clinic started as a small yet dedicated establishment that catered to 9-13 clients daily. Its commitment to superior care and expert services quickly gained traction, garnering a vast clientele. Now, with an impressive 1,200 clients and counting, the clinic boasts a new, state-of-the-art facility that caters to 20-30 clients daily.
